Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the VNM Bot ("the bot") handles information when it is used in a Discord server. The bot is operated by VNM ("we", "us"). If you have questions or requests, contact us at vnmbusiness@gmail.com.

Information the bot accesses

To provide its features, the bot accesses the following data from Discord:

  • Account identifiers — Discord user IDs, usernames, and avatars.
  • Server & role data — the members of a server and their roles, so the bot can perform role and channel management.
  • Message content of commands — the bot reads messages that begin with its command prefix (!) so it can respond to commands. It does not read or store ordinary conversation.
  • Voice channel presence — which voice channels have members and how many, used for voice-channel tracking.
  • Direct messages it sends — the bot may send direct messages (for example, notifications to team captains).

What we store, and where

  • Operational logs — the bot keeps short-lived logs on its host machine recording actions taken and the Discord user ID/username involved, for debugging and abuse prevention. These logs rotate and are overwritten automatically.
  • Voice-channel tracking (Google Sheets) — when the voice-tracking feature is used, only aggregate data is written to a Google Sheet: the voice channel name and the number of people in it. No individual member identities are written.
  • Tournament / event configuration — settings for tournaments and events created through the bot are stored as local configuration files on the host.
  • Game statistics — when a stats command is used, player statistics are fetched live from a third-party game-stats service and shown in Discord. The bot does not build or keep player profiles.

The bot does not collect payment information, does not read private messages sent to it beyond command handling, and does not sell data or use it for advertising.

Third-party services

The bot relies on a small number of services to function:

  • Discord — the platform the bot operates on, governed by Discord's own Privacy Policy.
  • Google (Sheets & Drive) — used to record aggregate voice-channel data and to export tournament files.
  • A third-party game-stats provider — queried live when a stats command is used.

Data retention

Operational logs rotate and are overwritten automatically. Voice-channel data in Google Sheets is overwritten on each update. Tournament/event configuration is kept only as long as it is needed to run the relevant event. We do not retain data longer than necessary for the bot to function.

Your choices and deletion requests

You can request that we delete data associated with you or your server, or ask a question about your data, by emailing vnmbusiness@gmail.com. Removing the bot from a server stops all further data access for that server.

Children

The bot is not directed to children. Use of Discord and the bot is subject to Discord's minimum age requirements.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
